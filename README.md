# 🦥 LAZY LISTER PRO: USER MANUAL

## 📔 WHAT IS THIS?
This is your "Automated Listing Office." It uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze inventory photos and write professional descriptions. It is part of the 400-series Ledger for your records.

## 🏗️ HOW THE FILES WORK (The "Anatomy")
* **app.py**: The "Manager." This is the main program.
* **.streamlit/secrets.toml**: The "Safe." Holds your private API Key.
* **requirements.txt**: The "Tool Kit." Lists the software needed.
* **.gitignore**: The "Security Guard." Keeps your secrets off the internet.

## ☁️ FINDING YOUR CLOUD BACKUPS
If your computer ever fails, your work is mirrored in your Cloud.
1. Open **Finder** on your Mac.
2. Look in your **Cloud Storage** sidebar.
3. Locate the folder: `.../410_LAZY_LISTER_APP/`
4. This folder is a "Live Mirror"—any change you save in VS Code is automatically backed up here for safety.

## 🧠 OBSIDIAN (Your Digital Brain)
Obsidian is where you store your thoughts, trust documents, and business strategies. It’s like a notebook that never forgets.
1. **To Open:** Press **Command + Space** on your keyboard and type "Obsidian."
2. **To Use:** Create a "New Note" (the plus icon) to jot down ideas for the Siro Trust or inventory notes.
3. **Why use it?** Unlike a regular document, Obsidian links your notes together so you can see how your business ideas interconnect over time.

## 🚀 HOW TO START THE APP
1. Open **VS Code**.
2. Open the **Terminal** (Top Menu > Terminal > New Terminal).
3. Type: `streamlit run app.py` and press **Enter**.

## 🔐 HOW TO SAVE TO THE VAULT (GitHub)
1. Open the **Terminal**.
2. Type: `git add .` (Packs the box).
3. Type: `git commit -m "Update"` (Labels the box).
4. Type: `git push` (Sends it to the vault).
